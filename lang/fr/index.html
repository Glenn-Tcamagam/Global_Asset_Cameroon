OUR LATEST CREATION: SYSDEVAM

OUR LATEST CREATION: SYSDEVAM, Dematerialization System of Securities

SYSDEVAM is "the most complete and best securities management tool on the market". 

It allows to generate in real time the final conservation statements (conservation register, conservation account, ownership certificate, dashboard of unclaimed shares, global issue account, general transaction log, dividend coupons, statements and account history…. SYSDEVAM is capable of exchanging data securely with any financial platform, thus facilitating procedures in the event of a possible call for funds on the financial markets..

QUALITY SOLUTIONS

SYSDEVAM makes it possible to manage all the transactions likely to affect the transferable securities in accordance with the provisions of the revised OHADA Uniform Act, the regulation of the Financial Market Supervisory Commission, Decree No. 3763 / PM relating to the dematerialization of transferable securities , of instruction note N ° 19 from the Central Depositary,
